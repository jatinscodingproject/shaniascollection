About Founder

Veena Joyce Madtha

Veena Joyce Madtha is a professional realistic artist with a Bachelor of Fine Arts (BFA) in Painting and a Bachelor of Arts (BA) degree. With years of experience as a teacher at Delhi Public International School in Doha, Qatar, and as the owner of a boutique in Bangalore, she brought her passion for art and design to life. Currently based in Mumbai, she specializes in creating custom paintings, designing and stitching dresses for kids and ladies, and providing personalized art lessons at students' residences. Let her bring your artistic dreams to reality!
